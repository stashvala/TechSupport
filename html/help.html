Help

FAQ

Q: What is a ticket?
A: When a client is having a problem with the software we provide it's our obligation to solve their problem. Client creates a ticket where he/she fills out a form to describe an issue at hand.

Q: How can I make a new ticket?
A: On the left in the sidebar you have to click "Tickets" > "New Ticket", then fill out the proper form.

Q: How can I edit the ticket?
A: Under the "dashboard" or "My Ticket History" you can click on the ticket number (ex. #123), that will take you to the editing section, where you can correct/add information, change ticket status(Opened, Reopened, Closed or Pending) and choose assignee/support level.
